Robin Fischer

PhD Researcher, WU Vienna

I am a PhD researcher at the Institute of Data, Energy, and Sustainability (WU Vienna). I am particularly interested in how firms adapt under uncertainty and shifting market conditions. I mainly work with high-dimensional, messy economic data, where I use Bayesian and machine learning methods to find solid ground and quantify uncertainties.

M.Sc. Economics (University of Cologne and Durham University); B.A. Mathematics and Philosophy.

Working Paper

The Widening Profitability Gap between Renewable and Fossil Power Firms in Europe

This paper examines how the profitability gap between renewable and fossil power firms has evolved in Europe over the past two decades, applying machine learning and bayesian methods to firm-level data.
