About Me

I am a PhD researcher at WU Vienna, under the supervision of Prof. Kavita Surana. I apply forecasting and control methods to enhance energy system models, focusing particularly on innovation and technological change. My research lies at the intersection of energy economics, complex systems and statistical modelling.
