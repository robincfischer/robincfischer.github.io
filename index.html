I am a PhD researcher at WU Vienna studying how firms adapt to the energy transition. My work uses Bayesian econometrics and machine learning to analyze firm-level data, with a broader interest in forecasting technology diffusion and economic crisis response.

M.Sc. Economics (University of Cologne and Durham University); B.A. Mathematics and Philosophy.
